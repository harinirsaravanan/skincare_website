Basic SkinCare Steps for Beginners




  • STEP:1

Always cleanse your face before starting your skincare routine. Wet your face, apply cleanser, massage in circular motions, then rinse thoroughly.

  • STEP:2

After cleansing, apply toner onto a cotton pad and gently sweep across the face, allowing it to absorb.

  • STEP:3

Dispense a small amount of serum onto fingertips, pat or massage onto cleansed skin, and allow it to fully absorb.

  • STEP:4

Following serum application, apply moisturizer evenly onto the face and neck, massaging gently until fully absorbed.
